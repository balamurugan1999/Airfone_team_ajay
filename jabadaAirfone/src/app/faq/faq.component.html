FAQ

How can i join Airfone ?

Please go to www.airfone.in and go to new connection menu. Type in your demographic details and upload your Proof.You would receive a Email and callback from the customer care executive.

How can i Know my balance,validity and data usage?

Sign up in airfone website and know your balance,validity and data usage

How do I contact customer care?

You can raise query by typing your query after loging into the Airfone Portal. In case you wish to contact customer care executive, please call us at 200 for queries/requests and complaints.

I want to know my current outstanding amount?

Login to the Airfone Web Portal and get your outstanding bill.

I want to know about my current package ?

Login to the Airfone Web Portal and get details of your current package.

I want to know the details of my last 5 transactions ?

You may check the details of your last few transactions by clicking the get history button in the airfone portal after loging in.
